### 31. If $\cos \theta = k$ and $\theta$ is an acute angle, then $\tan \theta =$

- **A**: $\frac{1}{\sqrt{1-k^2}}$. (Correct Answer)
- **B**: $\sqrt{1-k^2}$.
- **C**: $\frac{k}{\sqrt{1-k^2}}$.
- **D**: $\frac{\sqrt{1-k^2}}{k}$.
